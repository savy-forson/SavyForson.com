About!

Here you can get to know me a little better!

Hey, I’m Ira also known as Savy Forson.
I’m a freelance motion designer and video editor creating engaging advertising and educational content for digital platforms.
I design, edit, and animate videos that help brands communicate clearly, grab attention, and keep viewers watching.
I enjoy working on social media ads, typography-driven motion, and short-form content, but I’m always excited to take on new video challenges.
I love problem-solving and turning ideas into clean, impactful visuals that actually work.

In my spare time, you can find me drawing, playing games, or watching Rick and Morty, Breaking Bad, and Game of Thrones.
