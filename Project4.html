From Tired to Inspired

Ember Cafe presents a 15-second animated coffee ad that turns moments of fatigue into energizing enjoyment. Enjoy your coffee best with Ember Cafe!

Short Intro

This animated ad for Ember Café was designed to grab attention quickly on social media and connect directly with busy young coffee lovers. Many brands struggle to make a short message feel relatable and memorable—this project solves that by turning a simple daily experience into a story viewers can identify with.

The goal...

The goal was to capture the audience’s attention in 10–15 seconds, make them feel understood, and show how Ember Café’s coffee provides a quick energy boost. By using warm, smooth visuals and a clear narrative, the ad encourages engagement, increases brand recall, and highlights how the product fits naturally into everyday life.
In short, this animation turns attention into action: it helps viewers notice the brand, connect with it emotionally, and remember it when choosing their coffee.
